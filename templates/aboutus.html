About Us

We are dedicated to revolutionizing eye health through the power of artificial intelligence. Our cutting-edge technology, powered by Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), allows for accurate and early detection of a wide range of eye diseases.

How It Works


Upload Your Retinal Images: Simply upload your retinal images through our secure platform.

Learning Analysis: Our CNN algorithms meticulously analyze the images, identifying subtle patterns and indicators associated with various eye diseases.

Instant Results: Receive comprehensive reports outlining the health of your eyes, potential risks, and suggested actions.
